The Pinnacle of Alpine Craftsmanship

Step into a legacy of excellence. Our handcrafted Lederhosen, meticulously created in the heart of the Austrian Alps, embody the perfect balance of heritage, tradition, and luxury.

Forged through time-honored techniques, passed from master to apprentice within the Glas family for over five generations, our Lederhosen stand as a testament to artisanal mastery.

Each stitch, each embossed detail, and every carefully chosen material whisper of sophistication and authenticity, ensuring that every pair is not just an article of clothing, but a piece of living history.

Indulge in the excellence of bespoke craftsmanship. This is not just attire; it is a tradition woven into every fiber.
